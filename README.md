# Nexus Transit

**Smart, Safe & Intelligent Bus Tracking & Booking System for Bhubaneswar**

A next-generation transit companion designed to solve daily commuting challenges in Bhubaneswar that existing apps like Mo Bus (Ama Bus) fail to address effectively.

## The Problem

Commuters in Bhubaneswar face several serious issues every day:
- Buses frequently arrive overcrowded, leading to unsafe boarding and passengers being left behind.
- Inaccurate ETAs and unreliable seat availability information.
- Safety concerns, especially for women, elderly, and students — including harassment in crowded buses and rash driving.
- Lack of priority seating or clear guidance on where to stand for better boarding.
- Private buses often have zero real-time tracking or vacancy visibility.
- Poor user experience with slow performance and confusing interfaces.

## Our Solution

Nexus Transit is not just another bus tracking app. It is an **intelligent, predictive, and safety-first platform** that combines live tracking, AI-powered predictions, and user-centric features to make daily commuting in Bhubaneswar safer, more reliable, and less stressful.

## Key Unique Features

### Smart Seat Prediction & Safe Boarding Advisor
- Predicts how many seats will likely be available when the bus reaches **your specific stop** (accounting for passengers getting down at earlier stops).
- Provides clear boarding advice such as “Stand near FRONT door” or “Middle door recommended”.
- Helps users avoid the common frustration of buses arriving full or unsafe rushing to board.

### Women & Elderly Safety Intelligence
- Identifies and highlights **Women-Friendly Buses** with priority seating.
- Shows Safety Score for each route and bus.
- One-tap reporting for safety issues (rash driving, overcrowding, harassment) with live community alerts.
- Dedicated women and elderly priority boarding options.

### Hybrid Live Vacancy System
- Works for both government (Mo Bus) and private buses.
- Conductor-reported vacancy with clear timestamps.
- Combined with Nexus AI predictions to reduce the impact of misleading or delayed manual updates.
- Transparent display: “15 seats reported • 4 minutes ago” + AI-predicted seats at your stop.

### Dual-Path Live Map Visualization
- Displays both **Shortest Route (Blue)** and **Fastest Route (Green)** with smooth animated glowing lines on the map.
- Gives users instant visual clarity that basic tracking apps lack.

### Rapid Booking with Free UPI Option
- Instant seat reservation with transparent pricing.
- Zero platform fee UPI payment option for better user experience.
- Small commission-based monetization for sustainability.

### AI-Powered Insights
- Nexus AI Score for every bus (balancing speed, seats, and safety).
- Crowd and delay predictions based on time of day and route patterns.
- Smart recommendations like “Best bus for women commuters” or “Avoid this route during peak hours”.

## Why Nexus Transit Stands Out from Mo Bus

- Goes beyond basic tracking and occupancy to offer **predictive seating intelligence**.
- Strong focus on **women and elderly safety** — a major concern in Indian cities.
- Supports private buses through a practical hybrid vacancy system.
- Modern, clean, and fast user interface with beautiful dual-path map visualization.
- Transparent and trustworthy vacancy information with AI cross-checking.

## Target Impact

Nexus Transit aims to make public transport in Bhubaneswar:
- Safer for women and vulnerable commuters
- More reliable with accurate predictions
- Easier to use with clear guidance and rapid booking
- Inclusive for both government and private bus users

- Backend: Node.js + Express + Socket.IO
- Frontend: React + Leaflet Maps
- Real-time updates via WebSockets
- Hybrid vacancy system (conductor input + AI prediction)
